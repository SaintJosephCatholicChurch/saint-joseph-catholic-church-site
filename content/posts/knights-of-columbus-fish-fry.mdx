<p>The Knights of Columbus Council 7555 at St. Joseph's Catholic Church in Bluffton is holding an all-you-can-eat fish fry on Friday, April 5th from 4:30 to 7:30 p.m.</p>
<p>The menu will include all you can eat "Dan's Fish Fry" fish, and a serving of cheesy potatoes, green beans and desserts.&nbsp; Prices for the dinner are: adults $10; children ages 6012 $6; and children 5 and under are free.&nbsp; There will also be a drive-thru service available at the church, located at 1300 N Main St., Bluffton, IN.</p>
<p>Proceeds will be put towards Pro-Live and New Evangelization.</p>
