<p>St. Joseph's is hosting it's third Alpha this Winter on Thursdays from 6:30 - 8:00 PM at Family Centered Services (1515 N Sutton Cir Dr, Bluffton, IN 46714).</p>
<p>Beginning on Thursday, January 16th, the program will run for 11-weeks. Alpha is a course which introduces the basics of the <strong>Christian</strong> faith through a series of talks and discussions.</p>
<p>The evening begins with a meal at 6:30 PM, a video series at 7:00 PM, and small group discussions over the video series at 7:30 PM. Childcare is provided.</p>
