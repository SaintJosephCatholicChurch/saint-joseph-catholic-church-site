<p>St. Joseph's first monthly Young Adult (ages 18-40) gathering will be on Sunday, Dec. 15, at 5 PM in the Parish Social Hall.</p>
<p>The evening will offer a light dinner (BYOB), a short discussion on the Faith, and a chance to socialize (similar to the Theology on Tap model).</p>
<p>Free childcare will be offered.</p>
