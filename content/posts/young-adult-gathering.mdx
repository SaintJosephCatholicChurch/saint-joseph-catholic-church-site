<p>St. Joseph's monthly Young Adult (ages 18-40) gathering will be on Sunday, January 19th, at 5 PM in the Parish Social Hall.</p>
<p>The evening will include a potluck meal with the parish providing the entree (BYOB), a short discussion on the Faith, and a chance to socialize.</p>
<p>Free childcare will be offered.</p>
