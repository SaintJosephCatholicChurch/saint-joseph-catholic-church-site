<h2>WHAT IS OCIA?</h2>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Stands for the Order of Christian Initiation of Adults.</li>
<li>Is the Catholic Church&rsquo;s preferred method for welcoming new Catholics.</li>
<li>Weaves instruction, prayer, community/family activities and rituals into one process.</li>
<li>Provides on-going renewal that comes from grasping afresh the Church&rsquo;s Apostolic Faith and Mission.</li>
</ul>
<h2>IMPORTANT INFORMATION&hellip;</h2>
<ul>
<li>OCIA classes begin in September of each year and continues throughout the Easter season.</li>
<li>Classes meet in the Parish Social Hall on Thursday evenings at 5:30 p.m. (as of 2022).</li>
<li>Fr. David is willing to meet individually with those who cannot meet at the scheduled OCIA times.</li>
<li>There is no fee for the OCIA program as class materials are provided.</li>
<li>Instructions focuses on the history and structure of the Church, Scripture, the Mass, the Sacraments, personal and group prayer and&nbsp;foundational theology of the Catholic Faith, as taught in the Catechism of the Catholic Church.</li>
<li>Catholics desiring to learn more and to deepen their faith are always welcome to join.</li>
</ul>
