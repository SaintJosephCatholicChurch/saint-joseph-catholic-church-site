<p>Starting September 2019, Adoration of the Blessed Sacrament will be offered every First Friday of the Month following the 8 AM Morning Mass until 6 PM at St. Joseph Catholic Church.</p>
<p>At 5:45 PM, the Litany of the Eucharist will be recited, followed by Benediction and the recitation of the Divine Praises. At 6 PM, our parish's First Friday Votive Mass to the Sacred Heart of Jesus will be offered in the Extraordinary Form.</p>
<p>All are welcome to join us for prayer throughout the day every First Friday: At the Morning Mass, for Adoration, for Benediction, and/or at the Evening Mass.</p>
<p>Remember, time spent with the Lord is never wasted.</p>
