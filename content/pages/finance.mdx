<h2>PARISH FINANCE COUNCIL</h2>
<p><span class="relative -mx-px my-[-0.2rem] rounded px-px py-[0.2rem] transition-colors duration-100 ease-in-out">The&nbsp;<strong data-start="4" data-end="30">Parish Finance Council</strong>&nbsp;serves as a <strong data-start="58" data-end="79">consultative body</strong> to the pastor, helping to ensure that the parish's temporal and financial assets are managed responsibly, transparently, and in line with both Canon Law and diocesan regulations. It is our goal to ensure that our parish is always acting with</span> integrity, transparency, and strategic wisdom in all financial matters.</p>
<p>The Parish Finance Council meets quarterly, at times determined at the discretion of the pastor.</p>
