<p>Our Parish Council meets the first Wednesday of every month, August through May, at 7:30 PM in the Parish Conference Room. Meetings are open to all parishioners.</p>
<p>Suggestions, questions, and/or comments for the monthly meeting agenda can be shared with any Parish Council member or with the Parish Office (824-1380).<br><br>In accordance with our diocesan <a href="https://diocesefwsb.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/GuidelinesParishPastoralCouncils1.27.17.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><em data-start="107" data-end="148">Guidelines for Parish Pastoral Councils</em></a>, Fr. David has established the Parish Council to include at least six parishioner-members, each serving a three-year term. Each year, two members complete their three-year term. Nominations to fill vacant seats are held every June.<br><br><em><strong>Note</strong>: Following the loss of a pastor&mdash;whether through death, retirement, or transfer&mdash;the Parish Council ceases to exist until the new pastor reconvenes it. The new pastor may choose to reestablish the previous Council or form a new one.</em></p>
